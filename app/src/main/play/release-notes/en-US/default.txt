InkFrame 0.5.0 adds larger tablet and S Pen controls with clearer selected, disabled, focus, and press states; a refined Tablet Command Deck; contextual Timeline and Layer workspaces; and one editable Static Background shared across every frame, preview, onion skin, thumbnail, archive, and export. It also adds offline feedback reporting and stronger release/signing validation. InkFrame remains free, offline, ad-free, account-free, and subscription-free.
