InkFrame Studio is a free, offline-first 2D drawing and frame-by-frame animation workspace designed for stylus, touch, and mouse input. There are no subscriptions, advertisements, required accounts, or in-app purchases.

DRAW NATURALLY
• Stylus-focused Ink and Eraser powered by Brush Engine V2
• Continuous variable-width ribbon coverage with guarded pressure response
• Pencil, marker, watercolor, glow, neon, frost, smudge, and specialty brushes
• Adjustable size, opacity, hardness, spacing, response, taper, texture, and more
• Creative brush identities, deterministic Identity Mixer, Brush Match, and Brush Signature
• Profile Recovery, Quick Access presets, palm rejection, and stylus-only mode

CREATE ON SQUARE OR CIRCULAR CANVASES
• Per-project Square and Circle canvas modes
• Shape-aware drawing boundaries and transparent-corner exports
• Glass Horizon radial interface designed to keep tools away from the artwork

ANIMATE FRAME BY FRAME
• Organic radial timeline with circular or elliptical frame orbits
• Multi-ring timelines for longer animations
• Live frame thumbnails, S Pen navigation, orbit scrubbing, and hold-aware playback
• Per-frame layers with visibility, opacity, and blend modes
• Onion-skin controls for previous and upcoming frames
• Loop controls, motion effects, and playback from 1 to 24 fps

COMPOSE TIMING
• Direct frame-hold editing and radial loop handles
• Built-in exposure rhythms
• Reusable timing recipes and deterministic variations
• Exact recipe morphing
• Editable multi-recipe phrases and phrase library
• Complete timing scores and persistent editable score structures
• Separate 25-step timing Undo and Redo history
• Tablet History Inspector for returning to any retained timing state

CREATE AND EXPORT
• Import reference images
• Export PNG, GIF, MP4, and WebM
• Save and reopen InkFrame project archives
• Local autosave and a multi-project gallery

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
InkFrame Studio runs locally and does not require an account. The current release includes no advertising, analytics, remote artwork processing, or cloud upload. Artwork, preferences, and autosaves remain on the device unless you explicitly export a file.

FREE TO USE AND SHARE
InkFrame is released under the MIT License. The application is provided at no cost, with no subscription and no advertising.
